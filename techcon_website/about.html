Our History: A Decade of Innovation

TechCon began in 2014 as a small, local meetup focused on open-source software and ethical hacking. Hosted originally in a university lecture hall, it quickly grew into a major national event, drawing thousands of attendees and leading experts in the fields of AI, data science, and web development. Our mission has always been to be accessible and inclusive, making complex technology understandable for everyone.

In 2020, TechCon moved entirely online, setting new standards for virtual conference engagement, before returning to a hybrid model that maximizes both in-person networking and global participation. We celebrate our 10th anniversary this year!

Our Mission and Vision

The primary mission of TechCon is to **empower the next generation of technologists** by providing a platform for knowledge sharing, critical debate, and mentorship. We are driven by three core principles:

  • **Inclusion:** Ensuring equal representation and participation from all backgrounds.
  • **Sustainability:** Promoting environmentally conscious and resource-efficient technology practices.
  • **Transparency:** Fostering open discussion about the ethical implications of emerging technologies.

Our vision is a future where technology serves humanity in a sustainable and equitable way.
